My Services

My Services

A little bit of a lot of things.

Portraits

Portrait sessions have a very special place in my heart. Getting to work with a client one-on-one is beyond fun. I love helping clients find their angles and showing them how fun taking pictures can be. It is even better when we walk out of the shoot as new friends. Getting to take pictures of friend groups is a party in itself. I love being able to capture the fun interactions between the group and to capture memories that you will hold onto forever.

Couples

There is always a reason to get couple pictures taken. Some of the couples sessions I offer are engagements, bridals, maternity, just for fun, pregnancy announcements, and anniversary sessions. I hope by the end of the shoot you and your significant other had a fun, stress free time and will make getting your photos taken a new tradition. Let me take photos of you and your lover in a natural, raw, and beautiful way.

Families

I know that taking pictures can feel stressful to organize for parents, but something about family photos is so tender. The candid smiles, natural laughs, and fun sibling dynamics are beyond fun to capture. Being able to help you remember these precious moments is one of the greatest blessings ever. I promise that you will not regret having your family photos taken.

Dance Groups

My first paid photo shoot was for a dance group. It is so easy for dance photos to be filled with awkward poses, bad lighting, and to feel uncomfortable the whole time. I work to make it a fun experience for my dance groups. I will give you phenomenal photos with a very quick turnaround time. I will walk you and your date through posing so you do not have to stress at all. There is no need to question where to put your hands or how to tilt your head (I got you covered). Get ready for your favorite dance photos ever.
